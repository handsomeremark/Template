About Rodrigo Duterte

Rodrigo Duterte is a dedicated public servant with a passion for law enforcement, public safety, and social justice. Born on March 28, 1945, in Maasin, Leyte, he grew up in Davao City, where he later served as mayor for over 22 years. His leadership style is characterized by a no-nonsense approach to crime and corruption, which has earned him both support and criticism.

Education & Early Career

Before entering politics, Duterte earned his law degree from San Beda College and served as a prosecutor in Davao City. He rose to prominence as a tough-on-crime mayor, implementing policies aimed at reducing crime rates and improving public safety.

Presidential Leadership

As the 16th President of the Philippines, Duterte has prioritized key issues such as anti-drug initiatives, infrastructure development, and economic growth. He advocates for the rights of the marginalized and aims to empower communities.

Commitment to Service

Throughout his career, Duterte has faced challenges, but his commitment to serving the Filipino people remains unwavering. He continues to push for policies that foster national progress and societal welfare.
